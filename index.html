Welcome To Lone Tree Veterinary Medical Center

At Lone Tree Veterinary Medical Center, we believe that all pets deserve to have a good life. It is our professional commitment and our passion to help you keep your special companion healthy and active throughout its life, by providing complete pet care services—all at our facility in Lone Tree, Colorado.

Whether your pet needs medical, dental or surgical care, boarding, grooming or behavior training, all of your pet care needs can be conveniently provided in our friendly, pet-loving facility. Our team of veterinary professionals is dedicated to making your pet feel comfortable and special, treating new and returning clients like part of the family, and building a relationship of trust with both you and your pet. We open early, Monday through Saturday, so you can have peace of mind that we are ready to focus on your pet’s health care needs. As accredited members of the American Animal Hospital Association (AAHA®) and with several Gold Star Awards by the Better Business Bureau, you can rest assured that we meet and maintain the highest standards in veterinary care. Check out all of our services andgive us a call to set up an appointment for your pet today.
